Privacy Policy

Mushroom Run does not collect or store any personal user data.

This game may show ads provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These networks may collect anonymous information such as device type, language, location (approximate), and ad interaction to improve ad relevance. We do not access, store, or control this data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that such information has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.
